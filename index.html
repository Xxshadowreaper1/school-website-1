About us

Ysgol Rhiw-Bechan is a bilingual school. Children may receive their education through the medium of Welsh or English depending on the wishes of the parents. Equal status is given to both languages. To offer each and every child a wide balanced and differentiated education based on their ability and interest. We want to give each pupil an opportunity to develop and to prepare them in life and society.
